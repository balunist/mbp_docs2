PLUS Features Description
=========================

The MapBoards Pro PLUS upgrade is an **Advanced Productivity Suite** with a
**Next-Level Optimization Engine** designed for Autodesk Fusion users who need to maximize
material yield and streamline complex manufacturing workflows. It takes the base Pro 
functionality (3D to 2D mapping) and elevates it with intelligent nesting (TrueShapes), 
assembly design support, and enhanced post-mapping tools.


Key PLUS Features

- **Unlock TrueShapes Nesting**: Elevate material efficiency by enabling full rotation and
  part-in-part nesting to minimize waste.  
- **Advanced Assembly Support**: Enables Pro to function on complex Assembly Designs 
  (not just legacy hybrid designs).
- **Post-Mapping Control**: Rearrange boards after mapping to perfect the layout.
- **The Ultimate Workflow Accelerator**: Accelerates the leap from design to manufactured 
  CNC or Laser cut paths.
